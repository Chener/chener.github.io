WIldcards

* ? [char] [!char]

Examples
  • g* All filenames starting with “g”
  • Data??? All filenames starting with “Data” and 3 other characters
  • [abc]* Any filename starting with “a”, “b” or “c” and followed by other characters
  • [A-Z] Any filename starting with uppercase letter and followed by other characters
